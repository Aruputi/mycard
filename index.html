Mutual Fund Basics: Start Your Journey

What is a Mutual Fund?

A Mutual Fundis a trust that pools the money of many investors who share a common investment objective. This money is then professionally managed by an Asset Management Company (AMC) to purchase securities like stocks, bonds, money market instruments, and other assets.

Essentially, you buy units representing your share of the fund's holdings. It is the easiest way to achieve diversification and professional management, even with a small investment amount.

Power of Compounding in Mutual Funds

The power of compounding means earning returns on both your original investment and the returns it generates over time. In simple terms, your money starts making more money for you. The longer you stay invested, the greater the growth. Even small, regular investments through SIPs can grow into a large corpus if you give them enough time. Starting early and staying invested with discipline helps you benefit the most from compounding.
